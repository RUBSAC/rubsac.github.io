Signing up for the lectures

You will need to register by completing one of the forms below.

Once you have filled out the form, each week you will be sent an invite for the webinars you have chosen (e.g. Dive Leader sessions). You only need to fill out the form once, unless you would like to sign up for more!

Once you've signed up, please read the instructions page for advice on what to check beforehand and how to join the lectures.

 

Ocean Diver

1 Ocean Diver lecture/week (OT1-OT6) plus one revision session

 

Sports Diver

1 Sports Diver lecture/week (ST1-ST6) plus one revision session

 

Dive Leader

2 Dive Leader lectures/week (DT1/3-6/7-12) plus one revision session

 

SDCs

Compressor Operation, Drysuit Diving, Immersion Pulmonary Oedema (IPO)
