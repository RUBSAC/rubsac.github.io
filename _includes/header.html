About

As COVID-19 ravages the UK, RUBSAC have taken their lectures online and are offering them to anyone in the country who wants to listen. We're coordinating with BSAC HQ to ensure that the lectures can be recorded in your log books with a valid instructor number and code; all you need to do is the dives and you're all set!

All the lectures are free, and this web site will give you the schedules and resources you need in order to keep up with some training while we're all cooped up indoors.
