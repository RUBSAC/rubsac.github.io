Dave Ashmore

Open Water Instructor, Advanced Diver

Dave is the interim training officer for Reading University BSAC and does all the organising and planning for the lectures, including the liaison with BSAC HQ. Dave gives the Sport Diver lectures with Oli, as well as the Dive Leader lectures, and the Skill Development Courses.

Laura Bramwell

Open Water Instructor, Dive Leader

Laura lives in Exeter where she is doing a PhD in Medical Sciences. Laura helps to answer questions during lectures and gives keeps us all in check.

Oli Hancox

Assistant Instructor, Dive Leader

Oli is the current president of RUBSAC and helps give the Sport Diver lectures with Dave. Oli is a seasoned sailor and has lots of knowledge around boats, sailing and being at sea, which is very handy. He can often be found answering questions in the chat.

Chris Martin

Open Water Instructor, Dive Leader

Chris lives in London where as a paramedic he saves lives daily. He's often on hand to answer questions where his shifts allow, and is invaluable when discussing medical issues, oxygen provision, and rescues.

Drew Morgan

Assistant OWI, Dive Leader

Tweet to @dangerworm

Drew lives in Manchester but helps out RUBSAC and MUSAC whenever they need an extra instructor. Drew gives the Open Water lectures but stays behind the scenes in the other lectures, answering questions, giving out links and running quizzes.

Drew also keeps this web site up to date, so please tweet him if you spot problems.

Matthew Standage

Open Water Instructor, Dive Leader

Matthew lives in Oxford and works as a UX designer. Matt has been an instructor for many years and has a wealth of knowledge about all aspects of diving. He's usually in the chat answering questions during lectures.
