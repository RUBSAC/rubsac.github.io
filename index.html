Lectures Online

Introduction

Reading University BSAC club have begun doing webinars to continue teaching while COVID-19 has us all indoors. The plan is to cover all lectures from Ocean Diver to Dive Leader. All the sessions are free and open to everybody; all you need is a computer, an internet connection and either Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome. (Safari doesn't work but we're not sure why.)
