Lectures Online

Introduction

While COVID-19 has us all trapped indoors, there’s no reason to stop progressing your diving. For that reason, Reading University BSAC have begun to run all of the BSAC diver training program theory sessions as online webinars.

All our sessions are free to use and open to anybody from any branch and any diving agency. All you need to take part is a computer with Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (internet browsers), along with a decent internet connection. Unfortunately for Apple Mac users, our software doesn’t play well with Safari – you’ll need to install Chrome. To take part, it's quick and simple to sign up, click here to start!

For more information, please check out the schedule below or browse our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).
