What is that glorious hold music?

That, my friends, is Dave's hold music playlist on Spotify.


How do I record the sessions I've attended?

At the end of each lecture we give out a link to a Google form where we ask for your name, email address and club. Once that's done, the next page gives you an instructor number to enter in your log book along with a code that BSAC HQ assures us will be recognised across the UK.


I'm a PADI diver. Can I still attend?

Of course! We welcome all divers to attend and refresh their knowledge, or even just see how BSAC does things. If you're thinking of crossing over, there's a handy chart here.
